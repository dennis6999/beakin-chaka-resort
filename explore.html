Seasonal Activities

Rainy Season (Mar-May)

Lush green hikes, perfect for photography. The landscape comes alive with vibrant colors and flowing streams.

  • Photography tours
  • Guided nature walks
  • Bird watching

Dry Season (Jun-Oct)

Ideal for Mt. Kenya treks. Clear skies and moderate temperatures make this the perfect time for outdoor adventures.

  • Mt. Kenya climbing
  • Horseback riding
  • Camping experiences

Guest Tips

Pack Layers

Chaka mornings are cool! Bring light layers that you can easily remove as the day warms up.

Rent a Bike

Rent a bike from town to explore freely. It's a great way to see the local area at your own pace.

Bring a Camera

The views of Mt. Kenya and the surrounding landscapes are breathtaking. Don't forget your camera!

Plan Ahead

Some activities like guided hikes require advance booking. Contact us to arrange these experiences.
